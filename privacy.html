Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Brainy ADHD (accessible via brainyahdh.com), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

To provide a gamified focus experience, we collect the following:

  • Account Information: Email and name (via our custom authentication) to sync your digital pet and progress across devices.
  • Usage Data: Focus sessions, task completion, and pet evolution metrics to maintain your "streaks" and rewards.
  • Voice Data: When using the microphone feature, audio is processed locally or via secure API for voice-to-text conversion. We do not store or sell your audio recordings.
  • Identifiers: Device ID and IP address for security and subscription management.

2. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard partners to ensure the best performance:

  • Supabase: Our secure database provider where your habit data and pet progress are encrypted and stored.
  • PostHog: For product analytics. This helps us understand which features (like the iOS Widget) are helping users most, using anonymized IDs.
  • RevenueCat: To manage your subscriptions and ensure your PRO features are always active.

3. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your brain's data. You can:

  • Access or export your data at any time.
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated focus history directly from the App Settings. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

4. Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your connection to our servers is always encrypted via HTTPS.

5. Children's Privacy

Brainy ADHD is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
